# FUTURE_DS_01
A business sales analytics project using Power BI, focusing on revenue trends, regional performance, and product insights to support data-driven decisions.

# Business Sales Performance Dashboard

## Objective
To analyze business sales data and identify key trends, top-performing products, and growth opportunities.

## Tools Used
- Power BI

## Key Insights
- Technology category generates the highest revenue
- West region contributes the most to total sales
- Top 10 products drive a significant portion of revenue
- Sales show clear seasonal patterns across months

## Recommendations
- Focus on high-performing products and categories
- Improve sales strategies in low-performing regions
- Use seasonal trends for better planning and promotions

## Outcome
Developed a client-ready dashboard to support data-driven business decisions.
